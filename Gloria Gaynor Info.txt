Gloria Gaynor was an iconic figure in disco and soul music in the 1970s. Her biggest hit, “I Will Survive,” became the anthem of the disco craze of the 1970s and early 1980s.
This song was a tale of unrequited love sung to a driving disco beat and resonated with many to become a global anthem for female empowerment. Gaynor’s music, particularly this hit, helped shape the disco era.
