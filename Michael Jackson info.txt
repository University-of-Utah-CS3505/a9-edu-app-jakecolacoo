Michael Jackson is regarded as the “King of Pop” and revolutionized the music scene in the 1980s. His album “Thriller” became the best-selling album of all time, setting new trends in music with its unique blend of pop, rock, and funk. He transformed music videos into a credible art form, with his videos for “Beat It” and “Billie Jean'' becoming tremendously popular staples on MTV. His distinctive vocal style, electrifying dance moves, and pioneering use of music videos transformed pop culture and influenced a generation of artists to set a new standard for pop music.
